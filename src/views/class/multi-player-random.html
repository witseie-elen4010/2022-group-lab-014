How To Play

Single Player

-You have 6 chances to guess the 5 letter word
-Press enter to submit
-A green tile means the letter is in the word and in the correct position.
-A yellow tiles means the letter is in the word but in the wrong position.

Multiplayer

-The rules of playing are the same as single player.
-Multiplayer mode has 2 different options available.
-1)Players guess a random word provided by the computer
-2)1 player will choose the word to guess and the rest of the players will guess.
-This requires atleast 3 players to play.
-The first person to guess the correct word wins!
