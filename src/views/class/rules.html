How To Play

Single Player

-The goal of wordie is to guess the correct 5-letter word.
-The user will have 6 chances to guess the word.
-Type in the word on your keyboard or use the one onscreen
and hit enter.
-When the word is entered the tiles will change colour to show how
close the word is to the correct one.
-A green tile means the letter is in the word and in the correct position
-A yellow tiles means the letter is in the word but in the wrong position.

Multiplayer

-The rules of playing are the same as single player.
-Multiplayer mode has 2 different options available.
-1)multiple players each guess a random word provided by the computer
and the first person to guess the word within the 6 chances will win.
-2)1 player will choose the word to guess and the rest of the players will
guess the word.This requires atleast 3 players to play.
